1. BAWIPA A THA

Key: G

Bawipa a tha Bawipa a lian,
A thawm liannak mangbangza;
Amah hnenah a beutu pawl,
Ama’n him te’n in kilveng.

Ka lo thang`hat Bawipa ka thang`hat,
Na thawm liannak mangbangza;
Thuk in a sang lamdang duhdawtnak,
Ka beu lungpi Bawi Jesuh.

Lungthin kekkuai beidong mi pawl,
Nuhmei farah zonzai pawl;
Leivut lakin in khaisang ih,
Jesuh dawtnak lawng a si.

Thiang Thlarau in mit in vangter,
Dawtnak kutin in hruai ih;
In kawh tikah ka lo sawn ding,
Ka thuthup ka lo sim ding.

2. Test

Key: B

Sersiamtu Bawipa ka hrang ah hin a `ha,
Mi hmuahhmuah hrang khal ah a `ha ee;
Thleidannak um lo kan dinhmun tawk cio in,
In kilkhawi Bawipa cu a `ha a `ha.

Mi phunkip in cung Pathian hi,
Sangah hlir uh sunloihnak Ama'n nei seh;
Kan taksa `ulmi ni eng le zilthli thiang,
Man lo in in petu Bawipa a si.

Sersiammi lakah a kutsuak minung hi,
Huham nei danglam cuang in in siam;
Nitin te'n in kil tuu bangin in khaal `heu,
Bawipa cu kan hrangah a `ha a `ha.

Cangvai thei `ong thei thil tinkim hmu thei le,
Theihtheinak in petu khal a si;
Amah sunloih in kan aw neih hmuah suah in,
Sangsin ah cawi uh si Bawipa a `ha.
